This page is served by my Raspberry Pi which is running my onboard network.
The 'Store' icon opens a QT webengine and points it to http://mfdstore.navico.com/login/?and then a lot of details.
Using dnsmasq I point mfdstore.navico.com to the local webserver.
What works are simple pages. SignalK works half/half with the potential to lock the screen. It will autorecover(close) after 5 min or so.
I noticed no monospace fonts are installed, so these I had to provide myself. To maintain the option to Exit myself, I'm using a navbar in the top and an iframe below.
